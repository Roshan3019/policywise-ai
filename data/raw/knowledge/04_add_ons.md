# Car Insurance Add-Ons — Complete Guide

Add-ons (also called riders or endorsements) enhance your base car insurance policy for additional protection. They are available only with Comprehensive or Standalone OD policies.

---

## 1. Zero Depreciation Add-on (Nil Dep / Bumper-to-Bumper)

### What It Does
Removes the depreciation deduction on replaced parts during a claim. Without this, the insurer deducts depreciation before paying.

### Depreciation Rates Applicable (Without Zero Dep)
| Part Type | Depreciation |
|---|---|
| Rubber, nylon, plastic, tyres, tubes | 50% |
| Fiberglass components | 30% |
| Wooden parts | 5–10% per year |
| Metal parts | Based on vehicle age |

### Example
- Cost of plastic bumper replacement: ₹15,000
- Without Zero Dep → Insurer pays: ₹7,500 (50% deducted)
- With Zero Dep → Insurer pays: ₹15,000 (full amount)

### Cost
Typically 5–15% additional premium on the base OD premium.

### Eligibility
- Cars up to 5 years old (some insurers extend to 7 years)
- Only available for private cars (not commercial vehicles)

### Claim Limit
Most insurers allow 2 zero-dep claims per policy year. A third claim is settled with depreciation.

### Verdict
**Highly recommended** for new cars in the first 3 years.

## 2. Engine and Gearbox Protection Add-on

### What It Does
Covers internal engine parts and gearbox damage due to:
- **Water ingression/hydrostatic lock:** Engine seized after driving through a flooded road
- **Oil leakage:** Damage from lubricant oil leakage
- **Gear damage:** Internal gearbox malfunction due to accident-related damage

### Why You Need It
Standard policies ONLY cover engine damage if it results from a covered accident. Water ingression and internal failures are normally excluded.

### Critical Scenario
If you drive through a waterlogged road and your engine seizes (hydrostatic lock), the base policy won't pay. Engine Protection add-on covers this.

### Cost
₹500–₹2,000 extra per year depending on car engine size.

### Eligibility
Cars up to 5 years old in most cases.

---

## 3. NCB (No Claim Bonus) Protect Add-on

### What It Does
Allows you to make ONE claim per policy year without losing your accumulated NCB.

### Why It's Valuable
- Without NCB Protect: One claim resets NCB from 50% to 0% — you lose years of discount
- With NCB Protect: Your NCB is preserved even after one claim

### Example
- Car with 5 years NCB = 50% discount on next year's OD premium
- OD premium = ₹20,000 → With 50% NCB → ₹10,000 net
- One claim without NCB Protect → Next year NCB = 0% → Pay ₹20,000
- Savings from NCB Protect: ₹10,000 in first year alone

### Cost
Approximately 5% of OD premium.

### Limit
Protects NCB for ONE claim per year. A second claim still resets NCB.

## 4. Roadside Assistance (RSA) Add-on

### What It Does
Provides 24/7 on-road assistance when your car breaks down:
- **Towing:** Free towing to nearest garage (typically up to 50 km)
- **Flat tyre:** Tyre change service
- **Battery jump start:** If battery is flat
- **Fuel delivery:** If you run out of petrol (you pay for fuel)
- **Minor repairs:** On-the-spot minor fixes
- **Hotel accommodation:** If breakdown occurs far from home
- **Taxi arrangement:** Alternative transport if car is undriveable

### Cost
₹200–₹700 per year.

### Ideal For
- Frequent highway drivers
- Solo female drivers
- Owners of older used cars

---

## 5. Personal Accident Cover for Passengers (Unnamed PA)

### What It Does
Extends PA coverage to all passengers in the car (up to the vehicle's seating capacity) — not just the owner-driver.

### Coverage
- Death: Full sum insured per passenger
- Permanent disability: % of sum insured as per table
- Medical expenses: Up to a limit

### Coverage Amount
Typically ₹1–2 lakh per passenger.

### Cost
Approximately ₹50–₹100 per passenger seat per year.

---

## 6. Invoice Cover / Return to Invoice Add-on

### What It Does
In case of total loss or theft, the insurer pays the **original ex-showroom price** of the car — not just the current IDV (which depreciates every year).

### Example
- Original purchase price (2022): ₹10 lakh
- Current IDV (2024, 2 years old): ₹7 lakh
- Total loss / theft claim:
  - Without Invoice Cover: Receive ₹7 lakh
  - With Invoice Cover: Receive ₹10 lakh

### Also Covers
- Road tax paid at time of purchase
- Registration charges

### Cost
Approximately 10% additional premium on OD.

### Eligibility
Only available for cars up to 3 years old.

---

## 7. Key & Lock Replacement Add-on

### What It Does
Covers the cost of replacing:
- Car keys (including smart keys and remote keys)
- Replacing locks if keys are stolen/lost

Modern smart keys can cost ₹5,000–₹25,000 to replace. This add-on covers the full cost.

### Cost
₹100–₹300 per year.

---

## 8. Tyre Protect Add-on

### What It Does
Standard policies exclude standalone tyre damage (damage to tyres when the vehicle itself is not damaged in an accident).

This add-on covers:
- Tyre burst
- Tyre cut damage
- Rim damage

### Cost
₹300–₹800 per year.
