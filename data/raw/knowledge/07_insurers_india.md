# Top Car Insurance Companies in India — Guide for 2024

## Overview

India has 31 non-life insurance companies offering car insurance products. The IRDAI publishes annual Claim Settlement Ratio (CSR) data, which is the most reliable metric for choosing an insurer.

---

## 1. New India Assurance

- **Type:** Public Sector (Government-owned)
- **Founded:** 1919
- **Claim Settlement Ratio (2023–24):** 92.12%
- **Network Garages:** 3,000+
- **Strengths:** Oldest insurer in India, widespread branch network, strong in rural areas
- **Best For:** Value seekers, customers needing physical branch support

## How to Check IRDAI Data

- **Annual Report:** **irdai.gov.in/annual-report**
- **Bima Bharosa Portal:** For grievance registration
- **Insurance Ombudsman:** For escalated disputes

## Key Metric: What CSR > 95% Means

A CSR of 95% means the insurer settled 95 out of 100 claims received. The remaining 5% were rejected. Always choose insurers with CSR above 90%, ideally above 95%, for peace of mind.
