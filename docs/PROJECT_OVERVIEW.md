# PolicyWise AI
## Intelligent Car Insurance Analysis & Recommendation System

---

# 1. Introduction

PolicyWise AI is an AI-powered web platform designed to help users understand, compare, and select the best car insurance policies.

Car insurance policies often contain complex legal terms and conditions that are difficult for everyday users to understand. Many users purchase policies without knowing the exact coverage, exclusions, and claim conditions.

PolicyWise AI solves this problem by providing a smart AI assistant that explains insurance policies in simple language and helps users make better insurance decisions.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Car insurance policies are difficult to understand due to:

• complex legal terminology  
• hidden exclusions and conditions  
• confusing claim requirements  
• lack of clear comparison between policies  

Because of these challenges, many users:

• purchase unsuitable policies  
• overpay for unnecessary coverage  
• face claim rejections due to misunderstood terms  

There is currently no widely available platform that combines **insurance knowledge with AI-based explanations and guidance**.

---

# 3. Our Solution

PolicyWise AI is an intelligent platform that acts as a **digital insurance advisor**.

Users can interact with the system through a simple interface and ask questions such as:

"What is Zero Depreciation in car insurance?"

The AI provides clear explanations, recommendations, and policy comparisons.

The platform simplifies complex insurance information and helps users choose policies that best match their needs.

---

# 4. Product Features

## AI Insurance Assistant

Users can ask questions about car insurance and receive clear explanations in simple language.

Example questions:

• What is IDV in car insurance?  
• What is No Claim Bonus?  
• What does zero depreciation cover?

---

## Insurance Policy Comparison

Users can compare multiple insurance policies based on:

• premium cost  
• coverage amount  
• add-ons  
• claim settlement ratio  

---

## Smart Policy Recommendations

Users provide information such as:

• car model  
• city  
• budget  
• coverage preference  

The system recommends the best insurance policies based on these inputs.

---

## Claim Scenario Guidance

Users can ask real-world claim questions.

Example:

"If my car is damaged during a flood, can I claim insurance?"

The AI explains claim eligibility and policy conditions.

---
