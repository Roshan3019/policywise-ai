# 1. Product Overview

PolicyWise AI is an AI-powered platform designed to help users understand, compare, and choose the best car insurance policies in India.

Car insurance documents often contain complex legal terminology, making them difficult for average users to understand. As a result, many users purchase policies without knowing the coverage limitations, exclusions, and claim conditions.

PolicyWise AI aims to simplify this process by using artificial intelligence to explain insurance policies in simple language, answer user questions, and recommend the most suitable insurance policies based on user needs.

The system acts as a digital insurance advisor that helps users make informed insurance decisions.

# 4. Problem Statement

Many users face difficulties when purchasing car insurance due to:

• Complex legal policy language  
• Hidden exclusions and conditions  
• Lack of understanding of insurance terminology  
• Uncertainty about claim eligibility  
• Difficulty comparing multiple insurance policies

Because of this, users often:

• Buy unsuitable policies  
• Overpay for unnecessary coverage  
• Experience claim rejection due to misunderstood conditions

PolicyWise AI solves these issues through AI-driven policy explanations and intelligent recommendations.

# 8. Core Features

## 8.1 AI Insurance Assistant

Users can ask questions about car insurance policies.

Examples:

• What is IDV in car insurance?  
• What is zero depreciation cover?  
• What is No Claim Bonus?  

The AI responds with simple explanations.

---

## 8.2 Policy Recommendation System

Users provide information such as:

• car model  
• city  
• budget  
• coverage preference  

The system recommends the best insurance policies.

---

## 8.3 Policy Comparison Tool

Users can compare insurance policies based on:

• premium price  
• coverage amount  
• add-ons  
• claim settlement ratio  

---

## 8.4 Insurance Term Explainer

Users can search for definitions of insurance terminology.

Examples:

• Deductible  
• IDV  
• Add-ons  
• Cashless Garage  

The system provides simplified explanations.

---

## 8.5 Claim Scenario Guidance

Users can ask claim-related questions.

Example:

"If my car is damaged during a flood, can I claim insurance?"

The AI explains claim eligibility conditions.
